Scrambled Eggs

Description

The scrambled eggs, just 1 step from the most advanced egg cooking in the world, obtain a delicious and filling plate in just 5 minutes!

This eggs are perfect combined with sliced mozzarella cheese, and between two breads can make a healthy super sandwich breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs.
  • 5 ml of olive oil
  • 3 grams of salt.
  • A pinch of freshly ground black pepper.
  • Toppings! (Sliced sausage or meats are great)

Steps

  1. Use a fork to whisk your eggs and the salt in a bowl plate.
  2. Whisk until get and homogeneous mix and add the toppings.
  3. Preheat olive oil in a pan with medium fire for 1 minute.
  4. Pour in the egg mixture and let it cook for 15 seconds.
  5. Star stirring with a silicon spatula lifting it from the border and over itself.
  6. Let it cook for a bit and keep folding it over, when stars to get slightly brown its ready to serve.
  7. Add a pinch of black pepper or other toppings on top of your eggs, enjoy it with some bread and a hot drink!

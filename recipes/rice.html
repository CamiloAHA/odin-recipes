Rice

Description

This is the most useful and basic dish you can make, is pretty simple but it opens a lot of possibilities by adding your favorites toppings to your rice.

You can mix this dish with fried eggs, fried meat, or it's also great with canned beans.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 leak
  • 1 Garlic clove
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil

Steps

  1. Wash and cut the leak in 3 or 4 big chunks, next wash the rice and let it rest in a strainer.
  2. Add the olive oil, the leak, and the crushed garlic to your pot and put it in mid-low fire.
  3. Cook the leak and the garlic for 1 minute until it gets a light-brown color.
  4. Add the water and the salt and mix everything.
  5. Add the rice and put the fire in medium-high
  6. Check your rice every five minutes and remove the garlic and leaks when the water level drops.
  7. If no more water is visible, then low down the fire to low temperature.
  8. Move the rice with a stirring spoon and remove leeks leftovers if you see them, let it cook for 5 minutes more then turn off your stove.
  9. Enjoy your rice!

Pizza

Description

Pizza! The simple but powerful recipe that will allow you to have delicious sunday dinners for a lifetime. Did you really what to miss this one out?

The key for and excellent pizza is just one thing: the dough. It's also the hardest step, for beginners we recommend to use pre-made mixes, but if you are focus to get it 100% homemade you need to iterate over the dough step to get the mix just to your preference.

Ingredients

  • Your favorite toppings! We will be using pepperoni and black olives.
  • 200g of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  • 100ml of olive oil.
  • Pizza tomato sauce.
  • 1 package of pre-made pizza flour or:
    • 300g of all-purpose flour.
    • 7 grams of sugar.
    • 7 grams instant yeast.
    • 3.5 grams of salt.

Steps

Important: Prepare the dough at least 45 minutes ahead to give it time to rest and grow.

If you are using a pre-made mix just follow the instruction in the package and skip to step number four, otherwise follow from beginning:

  1. Mix 125 grams of flour with the yeast, sugar, and salt. Then add 10ml of oil and 175ml of warm water(Ideally 45℃) and stir it with a spoon.
  2. Gradually add other 125 grams of flour and more as you needed. Keep stirring until the dough is forming into a elastic ball and can be handle with your hands.
  3. Form your dough into a ball and transfer it to a bowl with spread olive oil. Wrap your bowl with plastic paper and let it rest cover from cold for 45 minutes.
  4. Pre-heated your oven to 210℃. Prepare your pizza pan by brushing it with olive oil.
  5. Get some flour into your hands and lightly on a surface, transfer your dough to the surface and knead it until gets cohesive and smooth, then form your dough into a circle and transfer it to your pizza pan.
  6. Spread your tomato sauce on top of the pizza and finish it by adding cheese and your selected toppings. Put your pizza in the pre-heated oven for approximately 15 minutes until the top gets toasty.
  7. Slice and serve. Enjoy your pizza!
